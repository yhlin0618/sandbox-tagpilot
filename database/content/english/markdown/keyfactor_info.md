#### Description

When customers select products, they usually pay special attention to some important product features, which are called "**Key Factors**". Your company's **brand** (Variation) **score** represents the performance on these important features.

You can refer to the brand score table above to see how each product feature scores, whether it's higher or lower than **Ideal** (which is the average customer rating for that product feature). This helps you understand which areas perform well and which parts still need improvement.
