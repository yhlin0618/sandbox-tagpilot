#### Description

This table presents your company's various brands (Variation) customer ratings on different product features. Scores closer to 1 indicate worse performance; closer to 5 indicates better performance. Based on these ratings, you can understand customer opinions of each brand on different features, and further evaluate whether they meet your expectations and which items may need strengthening or improvement. Blank fields in the table indicate that in the customer review data you provided, that brand has not yet received ratings for this feature.
